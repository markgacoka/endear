Frequently Asked Questions

Endear is an always free-to-use platform for finding your secret crush at Minerva. The platform is made by Minervans for Minervans and is founded on privacy, safety and mindful social connection. To use the website, you simply log in with your Minerva email, adding your crush and see whether your crush likes you back.

We believe security is in solid engineering. Therefore, we have reduced the number of moving parts by using social sign on, secure form submission and incorporated security checks during deployment. All activity on the website such as the types of requests are logged and monitored to detect malicious entry when it happens.

We only collect your email when authenticating as well as your first and last name for displaying to you and other users. The information about your matches are stored as hashes and cross-referenced to our database of emails for verification. So only you and your crush know that you've matched.

Do not fret! Since the website is in its early stages, as more users enter the site, the more your search space and potential crushes will increase. Alternatively, you can start a friendly interaction with Minerva students who are not in your friend group and it could be a match made in heaven. Read our solution on how we plan on approaching this.

The website provides an option to set a date through Google Calendar (automatic calendar invites coming soon) after matching to set up a date with together.

There are certain constraints set in place to minimize spam as much as possible.

Firstly, only Minerva based emails are allowed for authentication i.e. @uni.minerva.edu.

Secondly, each user can match upto a maximum of 3 other students and can make upto 3 changes in a 24 hour period. This is to ensure that a user cannot bruteforce their hidden matches.

Lastly, you can't see your if a user has added you as a crush until you have added them as your crush also.
