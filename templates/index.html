What is Endear?
Endear is an online platform made by Minervans for Minervans.

How do you ensure security of the platform?
The emails are stored as SHA-256 hashes and cross-checked against our database of emails to verify who your crush is. If the user details are compromised, the attacker will only have access to the hash of your crush's email. The attacker will not only need to crack the hashes, but they will also need to get the salt used to generate it.
